The Cast of Full House

The Tanners

  • Danny Tanner, patriarch of the Family and recent widower.

  • Pam Tanner, Dannny's wife.

  • DJ Tanner, the eldest of Danny's three daughters.

  • Stephanie Tanner, the middle child.

  • Michelle Tanner, baby with 'tude as played by the Olsen twins.

Extended Family

  • Jesse Katsopolis, Pam's brother, and benevolent uncle to the Tanner children.

  • Joey Gladstone, Danny's best friend and a professional comedian, also a doting 'uncle'.

  • Rebecca Donaldson, Danny's coworker and Uncle Jesse's wife (Season 4 Valetine's Day ep)
